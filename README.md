# gwlyu.github.io

This is the repository for my personal academic webpage, hosted on **GitHub Pages**.

Visit the site: [https://gwlyu.github.io](https://gwlyu.github.io)

## Overview

The website serves as a platform to document and share my academic journey, research activities, and personal reflections. It includes the following sections:

- **About**: A brief introduction to who I am, including academic background and interests.
- **Research Projects**: Details of ongoing and completed research projects.
- **Weekly Logs**: Regular updates on weekly progress, tasks, and milestones.
- **Notes & Reflections**: Personal thoughts, technical notes, and reflections on research and learning.
